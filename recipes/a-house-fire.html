A House Fire

Description

If you want to get out of cooking a meal for a loved (or unloved) one, making a house fire is a surefire way to do so.

Serves: a household

Time to make: 5-20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Any sort of compressed canister e.g. hair spray, deoderant, etc.
  • An oven

Steps

  1. Turn oven on its highest setting.
  2. Put object inside oven.
  3. Wait.
