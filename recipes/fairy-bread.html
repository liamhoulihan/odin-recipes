Fairy Bread

Description

Fairy Bread is a popular party food in modern Antipodean culture. Its major strength is its high wow-factor to prep-time ratio.

Serves: 10

Time to make: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1x White Bread
  • 1x Butter (or alternative)
  • 10000x Rainbow Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Apply butter to face of each bread slice.
  2. Cut slices in halve diagonally into triangles.
  3. Cover generously with rainbow sprinkles.
