Frozen Pomegranate Kernels

Description

Pomegrante seeds are sort of messy when you eat them dry. I like to serve them ice-cold.

Serves: 4

Time to make: 5 minutes (plus 2 hours wait time)

Ingredients

  • 1x Whole pomegrante

Steps

  1. Over a wide bowl, cut a small incision around the top of the pomegranate.
  2. Lift off the top, then cut into segments.
  3. With a spoon (or your hands) hit the segments on the skin-side to release the seeds.
  4. Spoon loose seeds into a plastic container and sit in the fridge.
  5. Remove after 2hrs for consumption.
